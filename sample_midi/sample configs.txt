Almost any midi file that has just piano, like acoustic-grand-piano acoustic-grand-piano-(bass), can use harp for the first track and bass for the second.

acoustic-grand-piano:harp acoustic-grand-piano-(bass):bass

This goes for:
sample_midi/lastchristmas.mid
sample_midi/bubbletea.mid
sample_midi/demon.mid
sample_midi/golden.mid
sample_midi/dragon.mid
